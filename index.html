      The Rules

      Objective

      The Townspeople win by eliminating all Mafia members. The Mafia wins when they equal or outnumber the Townspeople.

      The Roles

      • 🔪 Mafia: A member of the family. Each night, the Mafia secretly chooses one person to eliminate.
      • ⭐ Sheriff: A lawman in a lawless town. Each night, you may investigate one person to see if they are Mafia or not.
      • ⚕️ Doctor: A back-alley surgeon. Each night, you may choose one person to save. If the Mafia targets that person, they will survive.
      • 👤 Townsperson: An ordinary citizen caught in the crossfire. Your only tool is your intuition. Use the daytime to find and vote out the Mafia.

      Game Flow

      The game alternates between Night and Day. At night, the Mafia, Sheriff, and Doctor perform their actions. During the day, all players discuss and vote to eliminate one suspect.
